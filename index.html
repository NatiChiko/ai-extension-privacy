Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Trading AI Assistant ("we," "us," or "our") Chrome extension handles your data. By installing and using our extension, you agree to this policy.

Data Collection & Use

We do not collect, store, or share personal information. Our extension uses public APIs to fetch real-time financial data for the sole purpose of displaying insights.

Data Sources

We use publicly available third-party APIs:

  • Yahoo Finance – for stock, earnings, and crypto data
  • News API – for financial news and sentiment
  • FRED – for economic indicators and events
  • OpenAI – for AI-generated insights from screenshots

Data Security

All communication with APIs is done securely using HTTPS. No user data is stored or retained on our servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No tracking of browsing history
  • No storage of personally identifiable information (PII)
  • No data shared or sold to third parties
